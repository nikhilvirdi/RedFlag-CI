The Problem

AI models write code, but they don't know security.

Copilots and LLMs are accelerating shipping speeds by 4x, but they frequently introduce legacy patterns, hardcoded secrets, and injection points. Manual review can't keep up with the volume of AI-generated commits.

  • Security teams are the bottleneck for deployment speed.
  • Critical vulnerabilities reaching production via 'trusted' AI code.
The Solution

The Guardrail for the AI Era.

We provide an autonomous security layer that operates at the speed of AI. Every PR is analyzed, risks are scored with high-precision engines, and remediation is offered instantly.

  • Eliminate 90% of manual security reviews for common risks.
  • Scale security expertise across thousands of repositories.
